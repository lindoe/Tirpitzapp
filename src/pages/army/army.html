Army of Concrete

Walking along the West Coast, you’ll find them everywhere:
-Bunkers from the Second World War.
-Remnants of the Atlantic Wall, once built by the Germans as ‘An Army of Concrete’ to defend the Western Front.
Here you’ll discover the bunkers, but you’ll also meet the Danes and Germans who lived, loved, worked, and played here.

True stories about ordinary people who lived in extraordinary times.
